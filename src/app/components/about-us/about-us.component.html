Who is Purattu

Purattu Film Production was established in the center of two different worlds - Toronto and Baghdad, with a sole focus to create thought-provoking independent films that illuminate society's complexities, dive deep into the very essence of humanity, and entertain the souls. Most of our projects are international co-productions among Canada, Europe and the Middle East with production locations and talent from around the globe.
